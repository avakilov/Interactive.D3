From Players to Teams: Performance Trends and Aging Patterns in Baseball History

Interactive visualization explores how baseball teams performance changes over time. Using historical team-season data, it shows how offensive production, batting, and pitching evolve across years. Users can filter by league, select specific teams, and adjust the year range to focus on particular eras of baseball history. Hover over points on the line to see more details for each season.
